3 计算题

算符的平均值$\bar O=\bra{\psi} O\ket{\psi}=\bra{nlm} O\ket{nlm}$

1）

  1. $E=-\frac{e_s^2}{2a_0n^2}$
    则E的取值有可能为$-\frac{e_s^2}{8a_0}$ 或 $-\frac{e_s^2}{18a_0}$ ，概率分别为 $\sum_{lm} |c_{2lm}|^2$ 和 $\sum_{lm} |c_{3lm}|^2$
    得，概率分别为 3/5 和2/5
    则$$\begin{align}
    \bar{E}=\bra{nlm} O\ket{nlm}=\sum_{lm} |c_{nlm}|^2 E_n=-\frac7{72}\frac{e_s^2}{a_0}
    \end{align}$$
  2. $\bar{L^2}=l(l+1)\hbar^2$
    则$L^2$ 的取值有可能为$2\hbar^2$ ,概率为1
    则$$
    \bar{L^2}=\bra{nlm} O\ket{nlm}=\sum_{nm} |c_{nlm}|^2 L^2_n=2\hbar^2
    $$
  3. $\bar{L_z}=m\hbar$
    则$L_z$ 的取值有可能为$0,-\hbar$ ,概率为$\begin{matrix} 3/5,2/5 \end{matrix}$
    则$$
    \bar{L^2}=\bra{nlm} O\ket{nlm}=\sum_{nl} |c_{nlm}|^2 L^2_n=-\frac25\hbar
    $$

2）

$L_z$ 的取值有可能为$\hbar,0,-\hbar$ ;
$L_z$自身的表象为$c_l$ ,即波函数可以表示为$\psi=\sum_l c_l \psi_l$
$(L_z){\rho \nu}=\bra{m{\rho}} L_z\ket{m_{\nu}}=\delta_{\rho \nu}\bar{L_z}^{\rho}$
得到$$L_z=\begin{Bmatrix}
\hbar & 0 & 0\
0 &0 & 0\
0 & 0 & -\hbar\
\end{Bmatrix}$$

由U是幺正算符，得到$UU^{\dagger}=1$
$$\begin{align}
\hat{H}^{\dagger}=(i\hbar\frac{dU}{dt}U^{\dagger})^{\dagger}=-i\hbar\frac{d}{dt}(UU^{\dagger})^{\dagger}=-i\hbar\frac{d}{dt}(1)^{\dagger}=0=i\hbar\frac{dU}{dt}U^{\dagger}=\hat H
\end{align}$$
故H是厄密算符

  1. $$\begin{align}
    \bra{m} x^2\ket{n}&=(x\ket{m})^{\dagger}x\ket{n}\
    &=\frac1{\alpha^2}(\sqrt{\frac{m+1}2}\ket{m+1}+\sqrt{\frac{m}2}\ket{m-1})^*(\sqrt{\frac{n+1}2}\ket{n+1}+\sqrt{\frac{n}2}\ket{n-1})\
    &=\frac1{\alpha^2}(\sqrt{\frac{(m+1)(n+1)}4}\braket{m+1|n+1}+\sqrt{\frac{(m)(n+1)}4}\braket{m-1|n+1}\
    +&\sqrt{\frac{(m+1)(n)}4}\braket{m+1|n-1}+\sqrt{\frac{mn}4}\braket{m-1|n-1})\
    &=\frac{\hbar}{m\omega}(\sqrt{\frac{(m+1)(n+1)}4}\delta^{m+1}{n+1}+\sqrt{\frac{(m)(n+1)}4}\delta^{m-1}{n+1}\
    +&\sqrt{\frac{(m+1)(n)}4}\delta^{m+1}{n-1}+\sqrt{\frac{mn}4}\delta^{m-1}{n-1}\
    \end{align}$$
  2. 当n=0时
    $$\bra{m} x^2\ket{n}=\frac{\hbar}{m\omega}(\frac12\sqrt{m+1}\delta^{m+1}1+\frac12\sqrt{m}\delta^{m-1}1)$$
    故$$\begin{aligned}a    {m}^{(1)}(t)&=\frac{1}{i\hbar}\int    {0}^{t}H_{mk}^{\prime}e^{iw_{mk}t}dt\&=\frac{1}{i\hbar}\int_{0}^{t}x_{mk}^{2}e^{(iw_{mk}-2k)t}dt\&=\frac{1}{i\hbar}\cdot\frac{\hbar}{mw}\left(\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{m+1}\delta_{1}^{m+1}+\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{m}\delta_{1}^{m-1}\right)\int_{0}^{t}e^{(iw_{m0}-2k)t}dt\
    &=\frac1{im\omega}\left(\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{m+1}\delta_{1}^{m+1}+\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{m}\delta_{1}^{m-1}\right)\frac{e^{(i\omega_{m0}-2k)t}-1}{i\omega_{m0}-2k}\
    \end{aligned}$$
    当m=2时，$$a_2^{(1)}(t)=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{4i\omega}\frac{e^{(i\omega_{20}-2k)t}-1}{i\omega_{20}-2k}$$
    $$W_{02}=|a_2^{(1)}|^2=\frac1{8\omega^2}\frac{1-cos(\omega_{20}+i2k)}{\omega_{20}^2+4k^2}$$
    又$\omega_{20}=\frac1{\hbar}(E_2-E_0)$，故
    $$W_{02}=|a_2^{(1)}|^2=\frac1{8\omega^2}\frac{1-cos(\frac1{\hbar}(E_2-E_0)+i2k)t}{[\frac1{\hbar}(E_2-E_0)]^2+4k^2}$$
